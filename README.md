## üîÑ Compare Fingerprints

Evaluate your browser's privacy level by checking if the identifier changes each time you open the browser, after clearing data, or in incognito mode. 

If you consistently get a **unique identifier each time (üü¢)**, it's a positive indicator for privacy. However, if the **identifier remains constant (üî¥)**, it's a potential risk to your privacy, making you more easily trackable.
